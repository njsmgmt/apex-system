How It Works
Three steps. Fully automated.
Set it up once. Let it run every day.
Step 01

Prospect & Connect

Every day Apex finds targeted decision-makers matching your ideal customer profile and sends blank connect requests automatically — no note, no spam, maximum acceptance rate.

Step 02

Research & Message

The moment someone accepts, Apex researches live signals — a recent post, a funding round, a product launch — and sends a personalized DM referencing that signal within hours.

Step 03

Track & Follow Up

Every action logs to a live Google Sheet dashboard. Automated follow-up messages go out 5–10 days later to anyone who hasn't responded. Nothing falls through the cracks.
